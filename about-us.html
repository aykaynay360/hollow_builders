Hollowsection Builders & Architect limited is a Nigerian based firm domiciled in Abuja, Nigeria. We specialise in residential properties development for developers and other commercial projects, design & build, interior design, home remodelling, project management and Real estate.
Hollow section was birthed in 2020, bringing together a great collection of people with strong design, building and Engineering backgrounds with a deep experience of creating tailored projects with a rich heritage of successful project delivery and providing construction solutions spanning over a decade;
Our team Led by BLDR. Ahamefula Laurence Chikezie (R.builder, MNIOB, MPM) with a decade of dedicated construction and interior design experience. Our team is proud to have worked on various projects across Lagos and Abuja. Ocean parade towers, Eko Atlantic pedestrian walk ways, Prime Water View Estate Limited, Paradise Ville, Abuja.
Our firm is hinged on a culture of continuous learning and improvement in serving our client better, developing a thriving relationships with other experts in the industry. We are extremely passionate about what we do with an emphasis on optimal functionality, meticulous attention to detail and the brilliance we bring in the overall aesthetics of our projects; creating a heritage of excellence. We do not just build, we communicate our unwavering values, committed passion, robust client satisfaction and ultimately your project delivered within a pocket friendly cost and timely delivery.
